Caleel Harris is an American child actor, a voice actor who is known for his incredible talent that he has shown at such a young age and undoubtedly he became the inspiration for millions around the world. Talking about talent, there are many big names come to mind, but Hollywood film industry has given us most-talented child stars as well who have marked their own identity in the cinematic world and that’s a big win for them that they have won the millions of hearts of people and became the admiration for youth. After completing schooling, he got an education from the reputed college/university and besides, he took part in various activities, plays, events to hone his gifted skills.

Career

Before starting his professional career, he appeared in a short film named “Eyes to See” which was released in 2010. Since then, he started playing small roles and appeared in various television shows.

In 2012, Caleel Harris appeared in “Think Like a Man” which was directed by Tim Story. While it features Romani Malco, Michael Ealy, Kevin Hart, and others played significant roles.

In 2015, the young American joined Nickelodeon and worked there as a voice actor. Caleel Harris worked as a voice-over artist AJ from “Blaze and the Monster Machines”.

In 2016, the young actor, Caleel Harris voiced for Clyde McBride on “The Loud House” (Season 3) for which he earned recognition. In the show, he worked along with Grant Palmer.

Also, he worked in the sequel of “Think like a Man” –Think like a Man Too”. Also, he appeared in several popular television films and series for which he got critical acclaim.

Apart from this, he appeared in the Netflix min-series named “When They See Us” which was released in 2019.

Also, he gave guest appearances in “Kirby Buckets”, “Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn” and “Game Shakers”. Fortunately, this became the reason for his popularity and he started working in numerous movies during his career.

Achievements
