Mahogany Hotel And Suites, Ibadan

 Posted on May 21, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  148 words  |  Aldo Pusey

MAHOGANY HOTEL AND SUITES is a 4-Star hotel located at No 4 Remi Alabi Street, By All Saint’s Church, Jericho Ibadan, Oyo. It is situated in the heart of town and enjoys proximity to a number of public and private owned institutions.

Interesting places to visit around the hotel includes: Agodi Gardens, IITA, Cocoa House/The First NTA Station in West Africa,Shorite, and the UI Zoo.

MAHOGANY HOTEL AND SUITES offers a number of spacious and tastefully designed rooms which come in the categories: Presidential Suite,Dilomatic Suites, Royal Deluxe, and Deluxe Rooms. Each of these rooms comes with top quality facilities such as flat-screen television with a full cable subscription, sofas, luxurious bed, wireless internet connection, and en-suite bathroom with shower facilities.

Mahogany Hotel and Suites is a top-class hotel in Ibadan, Oyo.
