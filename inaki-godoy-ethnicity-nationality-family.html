Iñaki Godoy Ethnicity Nationality

To talk about Inaki Godoy ethnicity, he is originally from Mexico, known officially as the United Mexican States.

From a young age, he demonstrated a keen interest in and a special talent for the arts.

He also participated in acting, singing, and dancing workshops and further honed his skills through courses such as Hollywood Stunt and Drama and Theatre Summer Specialty.

In 2018, he made an appearance in the web miniseries “Por la mascara.” He caught the audience’s attention in 2019 with his portrayal of El Chinos in “Sin miedo a la verdad.”

In 2020, he made his debut in the film industry with the drama movie “Animo Juventud!” which follows the story of four teenagers on their journey to adulthood in Mexico City.

In 2021, he participated in “Quien mato a Sara?” a Mexican thriller series that is an original production of Netflix.

In the same year, Godoy was announced as the actor who would bring Monkey D. Luffy to life, the main character from the “One Piece” manga and anime in the Netflix series of the same name. The first season of the series premiered on August 31, 2023.
