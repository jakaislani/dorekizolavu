We are deeply saddened to announce the passing of renowned artist Radcliffe Bailey on November 15, 2023. Bailey, known for his exceptional artistic talent and exploration of Black history and culture, has left an indelible mark on the art world. The cause of his untimely demise was brain cancer, against which he valiantly fought.

Exploring Radcliffe Bailey’s Artistic Journey

Radcliffe Bailey (November 25, 1968 – November 15, 2023) was a contemporary American artist celebrated for his mixed-media, paint, and sculpture works. His art delved into profound topics, offering thought-provoking insights into identity and the historical experiences of the Black community.

Battling Brain Cancer: The Cause of Death

Bailey’s passing was a result of his courageous battle with brain cancer, a disease that took him from us too soon. Throughout his illness, he continued to create art that inspired and moved audiences around the world. His dedication to his craft and his willingness to confront challenging subjects earned him respect and admiration throughout his career.

Tributes Pour In for a True Visionary

The news of Radcliffe Bailey’s passing has prompted an outpouring of tributes from artists, curators, and art enthusiasts worldwide.Fellow artists have praised his unique artistic voice, his ability to capture the complexities of Black history, and his unwavering commitment to his craft. The art community mourns the loss of a true visionary.
