The death news of Jamie Raskin’s son has been trending on the internet after Jamie released his memoir, Unthinkable.

Rep. Jamie Raskin, D-Md., went through two unimaginable traumas in a single week a year ago. After years of battling mental illness, his son Tommy, 25, died on New Year’s Eve 2020.

Raskin was working in the US Capitol with his daughter and son-in-law on January 6, 2021, the day after Tommy’s funeral, when a violent mob broke into the building in an effort to tamper with the election results. In his most recent memoir, Unthinkable, Raskin discusses his ongoing efforts to comprehend those two traumatic events.

The superhuman element entered at this point. Raskin took advantage of a lifeline offered by Nancy Pelosi, speaker of the House, and decided to lead the effort to impeach Donald Trump for inciting the riot that might have prevented the peaceful transfer of power instead of giving in to his unfathomable grief over the death of his son.

Who Was Jamie Raskin Son Thomas Bloom Raskin? 

Thomas Bloom Raskin was born to overjoyed parents who witnessed him come into the world looking like a little cherub with blue eyes. Tommy developed into a stunningly attractive madcap boy with curly hair full of charm and laughter.
