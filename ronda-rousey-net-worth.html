Ronda Rousey | Career

Rousey is a multidimensional player who achieved success in various sports. Initially, she started her Career playing Judoka at 11.

Further, she started representing the USA and became the youngest Judoka to qualify for the 2004 Athens Olympic.

However, she could not win any medal at the 2004 Olympics. Meanwhile, she won a gold medal at the World Junior Judo Championships in Budapest.

She made a breakthrough winning a bronze medal at the 2008 Beijing Olympics.

And after retiring from Judo, she debuted in MMA in 2010. In mixed martial arts, she participated in 14 matches and won 12.

Interestingly, Rousey won her first 12 matches without a defeat and was later defeated in two consecutive games.
