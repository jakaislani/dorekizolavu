The Meaning Behind The Song: Anointing Fall On Me by Ron Kenoly

 Posted on June 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  448 words  |  Martina Birk

Anointed with Power and Spirit

Anointing Fall On Me, a powerful song by Ron Kenoly, is a heartfelt cry for the power and presence of the Holy Spirit in our lives. The lyrics call for the anointing, which represents the spiritual empowernment and consecration by God, to fall on the singer, their hands, mouth, heart, and every part of their life. It is a plea for a transformative encounter with the Holy Spirit.

The chorus repeats the prayer for the anointing to fall, emphasizing the desire for the power of the Holy Ghost to manifest in the singer’s life. The song’s simple yet heartfelt lyrics capture the essence of a genuine yearning for a deeper connection with God and a surrender to His will.

Meaning and Personal Experiences

Each person may interpret this song differently based on their personal experiences and spiritual journey. For me, this song holds special meaning as it reminds me of a powerful encounter with the Holy Spirit during a worship service. The atmosphere was charged with anointing, and the presence of God was tangible. As we sang this song, I remember feeling a deep sense of surrender and longing for the Holy Spirit to come and transform my life.

This song has also been a source of strength and encouragement during challenging times. It reminds me that I am not alone in my journey and that God’s anointing is available to empower and guide me through any situation. It serves as a reminder to surrender my burdens and rely on the power of the Holy Spirit.

Album and Additional Information

“Anointing Fall On Me” was featured on Ron Kenoly’s album titled “Lift Him Up,” which was released in 1992. Known for his powerful and passionate worship, Ron Kenoly’s music has touched the lives of many believers around the world.

The song has also received recognition beyond its original release. It was interpolated by Aha Gazelle in the track “Vegeta,” showcasing its enduring impact on contemporary music.

Inspiration and Invocation

“Anointing Fall On Me” is a song that inspires believers to seek a closer relationship with God and invite the Holy Spirit to work in their lives. It serves as an invocation, a plea for an outpouring of the Holy Spirit’s power, grace, and transformation.

Ultimately, this song invites individuals to open their hearts and surrender to the work of the Holy Spirit. It reminds us of the significance of being anointed and filled with the power of the Holy Ghost in our journey of faith.
